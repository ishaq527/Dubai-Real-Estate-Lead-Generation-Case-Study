#Dubai Luxury Apartment Lead Generation Campaign

##lProject Overview

This project demonstrates a full-funnel performance marketing strategy
for a Dubai-based real estate brokerage targeting luxury apartment buyers.

The campaign integrated:

- SEO (Organic Traffic Strategy)
- Google Search Ads (High-Intent Lead Capture)
- Meta Ads Retargeting
- Landing Page Optimization
- Conversion Tracking (GA4 + GTM)

---

#Business Objective

Generate qualified buyer leads for 2–3 bedroom luxury apartments
in Dubai Marina while reducing cost per lead (CPL) and improving conversion rate.

Target Audience:
- UAE Residents
- UK & India Investors
- Age 28–50
- High-income professionals & property investors


#SEO Strategy

### Keyword Research Focus

High-intent keywords such as:

- Buy apartment in Dubai Marina
- Luxury apartment Dubai for sale
- 2 bedroom apartment Dubai Marina price
- Dubai investment property 2026

### On-Page Optimization

- H1/H2 structured content
- Meta title & description optimization
- FAQ schema markup
- Page speed improvement (4.2s → 2.1s)
- Internal linking to property pages
- Trust signals (RERA certification, testimonials)

### SEO Results (3 Months)

- +68% organic traffic growth
- 15 keywords ranked on Page 1
- 42 organic leads per month

---

#Google Ads Strategy

## Campaign Structure

Campaign: Dubai Luxury Apartments

Ad Groups:
- Buy Apartment Dubai Marina
- Luxury Apartment Investment
- 2 Bedroom Apartment Dubai

## Optimization Actions

- Exact & Phrase match targeting
- Negative keyword refinement
- Responsive Search Ads (RSA)
- Conversion tracking via Google Tag Manager
- A/B testing headlines
- Call & sitelink extensions added

## Performance Improvement

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| CTR | 3.2% | 5.1% |
| CPC | AED 9.8 | AED 7.2 |
| Conversion Rate | 5.4% | 9.3% |
| Cost per Lead | AED 181 | AED 77 |
